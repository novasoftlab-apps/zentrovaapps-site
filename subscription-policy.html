Subscription Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026 · Applies to all Zentrova Apps applications

This Subscription Policy explains how auto-renewing subscriptions and in-app purchases work across Zentrova Apps applications available on Google Play. It should be read together with our Terms & Conditions.

1. Subscription Plans & Pricing

Where an App offers a subscription, the available plans, duration (for example, weekly, monthly, or yearly), and price are displayed within the App and on its Google Play Store listing before you subscribe. Prices are shown in your local currency where supported and may vary by country.

2. Free Trials

Where a free trial is offered, the trial length and the price that will be charged afterward are clearly disclosed before you start the trial. Unless you cancel before the trial ends, it will automatically convert into a paid subscription and you will be charged the displayed price.

3. Billing & Auto-Renewal

Subscriptions automatically renew for the same duration and at the then-current price unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period. Payment is charged to your Google Play account at confirmation of purchase and at the start of each new billing period.

4. Managing or Cancelling Your Subscription

All subscriptions are billed and managed through Google Play, not directly by us. To view, change, or cancel a subscription:

  • Open the Google Play Store app
  • Tap your profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions
  • Select the Zentrova app subscription you want to manage
  • Tap Cancel subscription and follow the prompts
Cancelling stops future renewals but does not refund the current billing period — you'll keep access until the end of the period you've already paid for.

5. Price Changes

If we change the price of a subscription, Google Play will notify you in advance of the new price taking effect. If you do not agree to the new price, you can cancel before it applies; if you do nothing, the new price will apply at your next renewal, consistent with Google Play's billing policies.

6. Refunds

Refund requests for purchases made through Google Play are handled according to Google Play's refund policy. We do not process refunds directly, but you're welcome to contact us if you need help locating or requesting a refund through Google.

7. Payment Methods

All payment methods are managed through your Google Play account. We do not receive, process, or store your card or payment details.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Subscription Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact Us

For questions about a subscription, contact us at info@zentrovaapps.com.
