Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026 · Applies to all Zentrova Apps applications

This Privacy Policy explains how Zentrova Apps ("we," "us," "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use any of our mobile applications (each an "App"). This policy applies across our full portfolio of Apps unless a specific App states otherwise.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Device information: device model, operating system version, language, and general settings needed for the App to function correctly.
  • Advertising identifier: a resettable device identifier (Google Advertising ID) used to serve and measure ads.
  • Network and connectivity data: where an App connects to other devices on your local network (for example, discovering a Chromecast, Samsung, or LG smart TV to control), we access local network state only to establish that connection. We do not collect or store the content you cast or control.
  • Diagnostics and crash data: basic crash logs and performance data used to fix bugs and improve stability.
  • Purchase and subscription status: whether you have an active subscription or purchase, provided to us by Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store your payment card details.
  • Approximate location (IP-based): used only for showing ads relevant to your general region and for legal compliance (for example, showing the correct consent form under EU/UK law).

Our Apps generally do not require account creation and do not collect names, email addresses, or other directly identifying information unless a specific App explicitly asks for it (for example, an optional support contact form).

2. How We Use Information

  • To operate core App features, including device discovery and remote-control functionality.
  • To display advertising through Google AdMob and measure ad performance.
  • To process and validate subscriptions and in-app purchases through Google Play Billing.
  • To diagnose crashes, fix bugs, and improve App performance and reliability.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms & Conditions.

3. Advertising & Third-Party Services

Our Apps are monetized in part through advertising and rely on the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy practices:

  • Google AdMob — serves ads within our Apps and may collect the advertising identifier and other technical data to personalize or measure ads. Learn more at Google's Ads Policy and How Google uses data from partner sites.
  • Google Play Billing — processes subscriptions and in-app purchases on our behalf.
  • Google Cast SDK — enables device discovery and casting to supported smart TVs and streaming devices.
  • Firebase (Crashlytics/Analytics) — where used, provides crash reporting and aggregate usage analytics.
Your ad choices: If you are located in the European Economic Area, the UK, or another region with similar requirements, you will be shown a consent form before any personalized advertising is enabled, and you may withdraw or change that consent at any time from within the App's settings. You can also opt out of personalized advertising generally, or reset your advertising identifier, from your device's Settings app (Google Settings → Ads).

4. Children's Privacy

Our Apps are not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it. Our advertising is configured not to serve personalized ads to users on child-directed or mixed-audience surfaces where applicable, in line with Google Play's Families Policy.

5. Data Retention & Security

We retain the limited information described above only for as long as necessary to provide the App, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or receive a copy of information we hold about you, and to object to or restrict certain processing. Residents of the European Economic Area, the UK, and California (among other jurisdictions) may have specific statutory rights under GDPR, UK GDPR, or the CCPA. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

7. International Data Transfers

Because we rely on global service providers such as Google, information may be processed in countries other than your own, including the United States. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards recognized under applicable data protection law for such transfers.

8. Third-Party Links

Our Apps may link to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties, and we encourage you to review their policies separately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of an App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, contact us at info@zentrovaapps.com.
